Buttered Noodles

Description

Buttered Noodles is a quick and kid-friendly recipe that features perfectly cooked fettuccine noodles tossed in a delicious blend of butter, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Created by elohel, this four-ingredient dish offers simplicity and versatility. Optional additions like broccoli, herbs, or shrimp can be included for added flavor. Serve it as a delightful side dish or a satisfying main course alongside garlic chicken or meatloaf. The recipe has garnered praise for its ease and tastiness, making it a go-to choice for a quick and flavorful meal. Store leftovers in the fridge for up to five days and easily reheat.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package fettuccine noodles
  • 6 tablespoons butter, cut into pieces
  • â…“ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
Steps
  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Fill a large pot with water, slightly salt it, and allow it to reach a moderate boil.
  3. Pour fettuccine and bring back to a boil; stir pasta until soft (approximately 8-10 minutes).
  4. Drain and return pasta to pot. Mix butter, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper into pasta until evenly combined.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
