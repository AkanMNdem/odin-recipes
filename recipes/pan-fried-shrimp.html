Pan-Fried Shrimp

Description

Pan-Fried Shrimp is a delectable seafood delight that promises a burst of flavor in just 10 minutes of cooking. This recipe features small bay shrimp expertly pan-fried on the stove with a blend of simple yet impactful seasonings. The shrimp are seared to perfection in a generous portion of butter, achieving a tempting browned exterior. The addition of lemon juice imparts a zesty freshness, while garlic salt and black pepper contribute a savory depth. The final touch of butter adds a luxurious finish to this quick and easy dish. With its perfect harmony of simplicity and rich flavors, Pan-Fried Shrimp is an ideal choice for those seeking a fast, delicious, and satisfying seafood experience.

Ingredients

  • 8 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 1 pound peeled and deveined bay shrimp
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice, divided
  • 1 pinch garlic salt, or to taste
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
Steps
  1. Melt 6 tablespoons butter in a large skillet over high heat. Add shrimp; cook and stir in hot butter until browned, about 2 minutes. Reduce heat to medium-low.
  2. Stir in 1 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice, garlic salt, salt, and pepper; cook until heated through, about 1 minute. Remove from heat; stir in remaining 2 tablespoons butter and 1 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice.
